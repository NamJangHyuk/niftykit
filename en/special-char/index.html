Frequently Used

What is Special Character Copier?

Special Character Copier is a tool for finding any of 170+ special characters — arrows, math symbols, currency signs, stars and shapes, punctuation, roman numerals, circled letters, and Greek letters — by name or meaning, and copying them to your clipboard with a single click. Unlike most symbol pickers, characters here are shown at a large size, so look-alikes such as × and x are never confused. Every search and copy happens instantly in your browser, with no sign-up or installation required.

How to use it

Type a character's name or a related keyword into the search box — for example, searching "arrow" brings up directional arrows, and "copyright" brings up the © sign. If there are too many results to scan, use the category tabs (arrows, math symbols, currency, and more) to narrow things down. Click any character to copy it straight to your clipboard.

When it comes in handy

  • Adding arrows, check marks, or similar marks to a document or slide deck
  • Marking currency symbols in a price list or product description
  • Inserting math or Greek letter symbols into a paper or report
  • Numbering a list with circled numbers (①②③)

Frequently Asked Questions

Are these symbols free to use?
Yes. Every character here is a standard Unicode text character, so it isn't subject to copyright and can be used freely.
Can I search in other languages?
Yes. You can search character names in English, Korean, Japanese, or Chinese.
Do I need to sign up?
No. You can search and copy symbols immediately, no account required.

This tool processes every search and copy locally in your browser. Your search terms and personal data are never sent to or stored on a server.
