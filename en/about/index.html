About GetNiftyKit

A small collection of free, browser-based tools built to do one job well — no sign-up, no installation, nothing to configure.

What is GetNiftyKit?

GetNiftyKit is a collection of free web tools you use right in your browser — no sign-up, no install. It's built for anyone around the world to use for free, regardless of country or language. Small tasks like downloading flag images, searching and copying emoji or special characters, or generating a QR code come up often but are surprisingly annoying to find a good tool for, so each tool here is built to do exactly one of them well. New tools are added regularly, and every one follows the same rules: no forced sign-ups, no clutter, and whatever you type is processed only in your browser — never sent to or stored on a server.

How it works

Every tool runs entirely in your browser. There's no account to create, no server-side processing of your searches, and no data collected beyond what's described in our Privacy Policy.
